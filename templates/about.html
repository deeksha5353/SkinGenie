About SkinGenie

Your Personal Beauty Companion

SkinGenie was born from a simple idea: to make finding the perfect makeup products easier and more personalized than ever before. We understand that everyone's skin is unique, and finding the right products can be overwhelming.

Our mission is to simplify your beauty journey by providing personalized recommendations based on your unique skin type, concerns, and preferences.

Our Story

The Idea

Founded in 2025, SkinGenie started as a passion project by a group of College Students to help people find their perfect makeup match.

The Team

Our team of beauty enthusiasts and tech experts work together to create the best experience for you.

The Future

We're constantly innovating to bring you the latest in beauty technology and personalized recommendations.

Our Values

Personalization

We believe in providing tailored recommendations that suit your unique needs and preferences.

Inclusivity

Beauty comes in all shapes, sizes, and colors. We celebrate diversity in all its forms.

Transparency

We're committed to being open and honest about our recommendations and processes.

Sustainability

We promote eco-friendly and cruelty-free beauty products whenever possible.
